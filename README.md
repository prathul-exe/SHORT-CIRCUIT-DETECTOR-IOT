# Short Circuit Protection Project ⚡

## 📌 Overview
This project demonstrates a **short circuit detection and protection system** designed to protect electrical loads and power supplies from damage due to excessive current. When a short circuit occurs, the system quickly detects the fault and disconnects the load, ensuring safety and reliability.

The project is suitable for **educational purposes**, **laboratory experiments**, and **basic industrial safety applications**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Detect short circuit or overcurrent conditions
- Protect connected loads from damage
- Improve system safety and reliability
- Understand real-world protection techniques in electronics

---

## 🧠 Working Principle
- Under normal conditions, current flows within safe limits.
- During a short circuit, current rises rapidly.
- The sensing circuit detects the abnormal current.
- A control element (relay/transistor/MOSFET/microcontroller) disconnects the load.
- The system resets manually or automatically after fault removal.
